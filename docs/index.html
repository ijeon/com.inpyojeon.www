Muninn (무닌)

DC Comics, 2021

A 10-page special issue collected in Batman: The World anthology. Introduces the first Korean supervillain in DC Universe, Saba and features Batman's little quest in Seoul to retrieve a high-tech weapon stolen from Waynetech Korea. Stunning art by Jaekwang Park (Conan The Cimmerian, Kiliwatch) and late Kim Jung Gi (Spy Games, TLT).
